How It Started
Kajal loved making baked goods to celebrate her friends birthdays. She knew the joy a sweet treat could have, and when she moved back to Lagos in 2019 she decided it was time to make her dream a reality. Starting from her home kitchen, Kajal’s Cookie Jar was born
Where We’ve Gone
Kajal started by bringing the classic american cookie home. Since then she’s expanded her offerings to also include additional flavors, cakes, and brownies. She then went to the Cordin Bleu, and is now excited to bring the best of french patisserie to her selection as well.
